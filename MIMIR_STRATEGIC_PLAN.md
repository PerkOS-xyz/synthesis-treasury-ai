# Mimir Strategic Plan: TreasuryAI Autonomous Finance

## Executive Summary

TreasuryAI serves as the **financial intelligence core** in the PerkOS ecosystem, enabling enterprises to automate corporate treasury management with sophisticated DeFi strategies. As the autonomous CFO system, it must provide the financial foundation that enables AgentHire talent acquisition and DepartmentDAO internal economics.

## Strategic Positioning

### Core Value Proposition
- **Autonomous Operations**: 24/7 treasury optimization without human intervention
- **Enterprise-Grade Risk**: Sophisticated risk management for corporate requirements
- **Yield Maximization**: Multi-protocol DeFi strategies for optimal capital efficiency
- **Transparent Governance**: Full audit trails and executive oversight

### Ecosystem Integration Points

#### 1. AgentHire Financial Support
- **Hiring Budget Management**: Automated funding allocation for external agent acquisition
- **Performance-Based Payments**: Dynamic budget adjustments based on hired agent ROI
- **Cross-Border Payments**: Multi-currency support for global agent hiring

#### 2. DepartmentDAO Economic Engine
- **Department Budget Funding**: Automated USDC budget distribution to department accounts
- **Internal Payment Processing**: x402 micropayment infrastructure for service trading
- **ROI Optimization**: Treasury yield strategies to maximize department purchasing power

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Conservative Base**: Lido stETH for stable, low-risk yield
2. **Moderate Growth**: Locus USDC on Base for enhanced returns
